How to Play

Game Overview

Survive 5 rounds of riddles and AI challenges. Score points by solving riddles or completing challenges.

Round Structure

Riddle Phase: Everyone answers a riddle. First correct answer wins a point.

Challenge Phase: Non-winners face AI challenges to earn points.

Challenge Types

Negotiator: Persuade and compromise (70 seconds)

Detective: Solve mysteries with clues (40 seconds)

Trivia: Answer difficult questions (40 seconds)

Fast Tapper: Tap as fast as possible (10 seconds)

Danger: Survive emergency scenarios (40 seconds)

Winning

Player with the most points after 5 rounds wins. The Oracle judges your performance!
